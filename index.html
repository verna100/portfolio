Background

My Journey to coding has been an uncommon one but that seems to be the story for many aspiring developers nowadays. My story begins in finance. I graduated college with a Bachelor's in Business Administration. I went on to work at the financial firm where I interned and so began my journey to find my passion. I worked in finance for a number of years until I decided to follow my true passion and pursue a career in law. This meant applications and the LSAT. Once I decided this was my true calling I started looking beyond finance and found another job at what I thought was a law firm and ended up being a sports agency. I took the chance as the opportunity was too good to pass up and so begun my long career in baseball. The law school thing didn't pan out and I decided to put all my focus into my new demanding job. At times even while working here I was still lacking the mental stimulation I craved of constantly learning and being able to use new technologies and keep up with the times. Baseball, unfortunately is still set in it's ways. I left this job in January of 2017 with no future plans other than possibly land a job where I felt good about the work and better yet could be exposed to and learn new things. 6 months to the day I quit I started at Adobe and here I am!

Team Project 2:Hmwrk Bot

2nd Project tougher tech

This was my second group project. In this specific project we came together to help students like us. We created a homework slackbot with a corresponding admin page. This allows students to easily type in slash commands with the week's assignment to find out when it's due and get a corresponding zip file of the assignment. The admin page allows TA's to update the assignments seamlessly via computer or mobile. They can also add or delete assignments from this site. This project was fun and challenging as I took on the authentication portion. It's tricky as I first got it up and running with EJS and mid project realized it didn't work nicely with Handlebars so I had to scrap the use of EJS altogether and make it work.
